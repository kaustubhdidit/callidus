Timeline

9-05-2021
Foundation

The foundation of Callidus lead by Harshit Pandey and Aryan Shukla, marked a pioneering initiative within our college, addressing a void that had long persisted. In an institution primarily devoted to technical education, the absence of a dedicated literary platform became increasingly evident. Callidus emerged as a response to this gap, recognizing the pressing need for students to enhance their communication skills.

15-11-2021
OFFICIAL collaboration

This initiative to bring forth the literary culture to our college progressed one step further when it officially collaborated with the literary sub-council for the first time. Word spread about its impactful initiatives, turning Callidus into a sought-after institution. Official collaboration marked a milestone, affirming its status as a beacon for literary enrichment and personal growth.

20-08-2019
FIRST-EVENT

Callidus, in its inaugural event, hosted an online declamation that proved to be a game-changer for the club's visibility. Open to freshers and new students, the event provided a platform for participants to showcase their oratory skills and engage in impassioned discourse. The fresh batch of students, eager to explore extracurricular opportunities, found in Callidus a dynamic space to express themselves. The event's success generated considerable traction for the club, drawing attention for fostering a vibrant community of enthusiastic individuals.

20-08-2019
THE MUN

The Callidus Club orchestrated an exemplary online Model United Nations (MUN) that transcended geographical boundaries. This commendable initiative not only showcased the club's organizational prowess but also demonstrated its commitment to providing a platform for intellectual exchange. Gathering participants from colleges across the country, the online MUN facilitated a diverse and enriching discourse, fostering a sense of global engagement among attendees. This online MUN stands as a testament to Callidus's dedication to fostering a spirit of collaboration and knowledge-sharing and still stands at the top of the achievement list of the club.
